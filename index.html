About Cougle Property Care

Cougle Property Care is a Fredericton‑based lawn care company focused on helping homeowners maintain clean, healthy, and well‑kept properties. Founded in 2022, the company has grown quickly by providing reliable service, clear communication, and visible results.

Our team focuses heavily on lawn health services such as overseeding, fertilization, liming, and compost top dressing to help properties look better and stay healthier throughout the season. Whether it's weekly mowing, seasonal cleanups, or improving lawn health, our goal is to deliver professional service that homeowners can depend on.

We proudly serve Fredericton and surrounding communities.
